Use System Reports
==================

Pre-defined system reports are located under **Report & Segments > Reports** in the main menu.

.. image:: /user_doc/img/reports/system_reports_1.png

The following system reports are available out-of-the-box at the Oro application:

* :ref:`Account Reports <user-guide-accounts-reports>`
* :ref:`Leads Reports <doc-leads-reports>`
* :ref:`Opportunities Reports <user-guide-opportunities-reports>`
* :ref:`Best Selling Products Report <best-selling-products-reports>`
